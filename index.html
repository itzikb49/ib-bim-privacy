Privacy Policy for Get Linked IDs

Effective Date: November 2024 | Last Updated: November 2024

Quick Summary: Get Linked IDs does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal or project data. All operations are performed locally within your Revit environment.

Overview

Get Linked IDs ("the Add-in") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for the Get Linked IDs add-in for Autodesk Revit.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. The Add-in operates entirely within your local Revit environment and does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track usage statistics
  • Store project data
  • Access network connections
  • Transmit any data externally

How the Add-in Works

  • All operations are performed locally within Revit
  • Element IDs are displayed only in the application dialog
  • Copied data goes directly to your system clipboard
  • No data leaves your computer

Third-Party Services

The Add-in does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, including:

  • Analytics platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Cloud storage services
  • External databases

Data Storage

  • No data is stored by the Add-in
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No cache files are created
  • All operations exist only during the active Revit session

Children's Privacy

The Add-in does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no security risks related to data breaches. The Add-in runs entirely within Revit's secure environment.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Your Rights

As we do not collect any personal data:

  • There is no data to access, modify, or delete
  • No consent is required for data processing
  • No opt-out mechanisms are necessary

Compliance

This Add-in complies with:

  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws
